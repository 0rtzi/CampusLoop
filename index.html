The Challenge

Temporary students need a smarter and simpler circular marketplace

Every year, thousands of international students arrive in South Korea for exchange programs, language courses, and university degrees. During their stay, they need to buy everyday items such as furniture, kitchen supplies, electronics, bedding, and study materials.

However, most of these students only stay in the country for a limited period of time, usually between 4 and 12 months. When their exchange ends, they face a major problem: they do not know what to do with all the items they accumulated during their stay.

Why existing platforms fail:

  • Language Barrier: Many second-hand apps are only available in Korean.
  • Communication Barriers: Language gaps make negotiations and transactions harder.
  • Lack of Trust: There is very little trust or safety verification between unknown users.
  • Not Adapted: Current marketplaces are simply not designed for temporary student life.
Our Solution

A smarter way to buy, sell, and move on

🔒

Campus-Based Marketplace

CampusLoop creates an exclusive second-hand marketplace for international students, making transactions safer, faster, and more trusted through university verification networks.

✈️

Move-Out Selling System

We help you quickly sell your belongings before heading back home with simplified listings, campus-focused visibility, and special move-out packages.

♻️

Circular Student Ecosystem

Reduce environmental waste by allowing essential products to circulate continuously between incoming and outgoing student generations.
